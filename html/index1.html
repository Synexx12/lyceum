About Our School

Welcome to Lyceum Paradise Academy

Established in 2005, Lyceum Paradise Academy has been a beacon of quality education in Kathmandu, fostering academic excellence and character development.

Our institution provides a nurturing environment that encourages intellectual curiosity, creativity, and personal growth. We prepare students to meet the challenges of an ever-changing global society.
