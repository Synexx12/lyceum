About Lyceum Paradise Academy

Founded in 2005, Lyceum Paradise Academy began with a vision to provide quality education that balances academic excellence with character development.

What started as a small learning center with just 35 students has grown into one of Kathmandu's premier educational institutions, serving over 850 students with a faculty of 45 dedicated professionals.

  • Student-centered learning
  • Holistic development
  • Innovative teaching methods
  • Safe learning environment
  • Technology integration
  • Community engagement
